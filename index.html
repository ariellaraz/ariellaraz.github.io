RESEARCH OVERVIEW

Mathematical modelling of cytoskeletal signalling networks underlying cancer cell migration. PhD research in mathematical oncology at the University of Sussex combining live cell imaging with dynamical systems and reaction diffusion PDE models to identify minimal circuit structures organising lamellipodial protrusion and migration persistence.
