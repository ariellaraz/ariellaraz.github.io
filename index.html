RESEARCH OVERVIEW

PhD research in the mathematical modelling arm of the CytoNet project at the University of Sussex. My work develops systems-level dynamical models of cytoskeletal signalling networks controlling cancer cell migration, using experimental observations from collaborators to identify minimal interaction structures governing lamellipodial protrusion and migration persistence.

The project focuses on how directed migration, migration persistence, and cancer-related changes in cytoskeletal regulation can be understood through mathematical modelling.
